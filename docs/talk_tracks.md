# Sales Talk Tracks

## Fleet Fueling Opener

"Hi [Name], this is [Your Name] from Fox Fuel. I noticed your fleet operates [X] power units in the [Location] area. Many fleet managers in your position are looking for reliable fuel delivery that keeps their operations running smoothly. 

Are you currently working with a fuel distributor, or are you handling fuel procurement in-house?"

**Key Points:**
- Focus on fleet size and operational reliability
- Emphasize local presence and quick response times
- Highlight bulk pricing advantages

---

## Generator Reliability Pitch

"Hi [Name], I'm calling from Fox Fuel. I see you have [capacity] gallons of diesel storage for backup generators. When the power goes out, you need confidence that your fuel supply is fresh and your tanks are ready.

We specialize in generator fuel maintenance programs that include regular testing, fuel polishing, and emergency delivery guarantees. How often do you test your generators, and when was the last time you had your fuel tested?"

**Key Points:**
- Stress critical nature of backup power
- Offer fuel quality testing and maintenance
- Position as insurance against downtime

---

## Cold Storage and Food Safety Scenario

"Hi [Name], this is [Your Name] from Fox Fuel. I understand you operate a cold storage facility that requires reliable backup power to maintain food safety standards. 

A power outage at a food storage facility can result in significant product loss and regulatory issues. We work with several cold storage facilities in the region to ensure their generators are always ready. Do you have a fuel maintenance program in place currently?"

**Key Points:**
- Connect fuel reliability to food safety compliance
- Reference similar facilities in the area
- Offer emergency response guarantees

---

## Pre-Storm Assurance Script

"Hi [Name], this is [Your Name] from Fox Fuel. With [storm name/season] approaching, I wanted to reach out proactively. 

Many of our customers appreciate having their fuel tanks topped off and generators tested before severe weather hits. We can schedule a delivery this week to ensure you're prepared. When was the last time you had your tanks filled, and do you have a pre-storm checklist for your backup systems?"

**Key Points:**
- Proactive approach shows care and planning
- Offer immediate action (tank fill, testing)
- Position as risk mitigation

---

## General Follow-Up Questions

- "What's your current fuel supplier relationship like?"
- "How do you handle fuel procurement during peak demand periods?"
- "Have you experienced any fuel quality issues in the past?"
- "What's most important to you in a fuel supplier: price, reliability, or service?"
